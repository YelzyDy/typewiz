grimouge movement guide ;>

00 - 31 -> floating/flying normal form of the grimouge

32 - 46 -> second form of the grimouge {could be used alternatively when about to die or upskill}

47 -> back to normal {about to attack}

48 - 50 -> about to bite

51 - 52 -> mouth wide open

53 - 54 -> clamped out

55 -> mouth fully closed

56 - 58 -> transforming to normal form again

59 - 60 -> about to go on his dead state

61 - 70 -> paper ashes, poof dead xx
